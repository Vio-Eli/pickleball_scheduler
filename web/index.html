🎾 Pickleball Scheduler

Mixed-doubles round-robins where everyone partners and faces everyone with the most variety — and the courts stay full.

×

Full courts keeps everyone playing; most variety minimizes repeat opponents (a few may sit out). Balanced picks the better of the two.

Below the ceiling nobody repeats a partner or opponent; above it, repeats are spread as thinly as possible.

The last round may be partial to hit the count exactly.

Schedule

Runs entirely in your browser — the scheduling engine is compiled from Rust to WebAssembly. No data leaves your device.
